Water Storage

Farm Pond & Lining Solutions

Rajasthan's sandy soils lose stored water rapidly through seepage. Our multi-layer HDPE geomembrane pond liners (200–500 microns) eliminate this loss completely, enabling reliable year-round water storage for crops and livestock.

We supply, deliver, and assist with the installation of pond liners for farm ponds, rainwater harvesting structures, aquaculture ponds, and government water conservation projects.

Zero SeepageGeomembrane barrier retains 100% of stored water — no ground loss.
