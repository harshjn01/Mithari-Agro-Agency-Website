Water Saving Tips

Smart Farming Habits

🌅

Irrigate in Early Morning

Water early morning or late evening to reduce evaporation losses by up to 30% compared to midday irrigation.

🌱

Mulch Around Crop Rows

Organic or plastic mulch around roots retains soil moisture, cutting irrigation frequency significantly.

💧

Check Emitters Regularly

Monthly inspection of drip emitters prevents clogging — ensuring every plant receives its designed water volume.

📊

Monitor Soil Moisture

Use a simple soil probe or tensiometer to irrigate only when crops actually need water — not on a fixed schedule.

🏞️

Line Your Farm Pond

A properly lined pond can save lakhs of liters every monsoon season that would otherwise seep away unused.

Rajasthan Govt. Subsidy / कृषि अनुदान

Get Up To 70-75% Govt. Subsidy / सरकारी योजनाएं

The Rajasthan Department of Agriculture provides financial support for drip irrigation, sprinklers, and farm ponds (Khet Talai) to promote water conservation. Mithari Agro Agency is an authorized partner helping you clear all documentation easily.

📋 Documents Needed / आवश्यक दस्तावेज:

  • Jamabandi Copy (नकल जमाबंदी): Land ownership record (not older than 6 months). / 6 महीने से अधिक पुरानी न हो।
  • Naksha Trace (नक्शा ट्रेस): Land map certified by Patwari. / हल्का पटवारी द्वारा प्रमाणित नक्शा।
  • Jan Aadhaar / Aadhaar (जन आधार / आधार): Linked with active mobile number for OTP. / जन आधार कार्ड व आधार कार्ड।
  • Soil & Water Report (मिट्टी-पानी जांच): Certified lab testing report. / प्राधिकृत प्रयोगशाला की रिपोर्ट।
  • Electricity Bill / Certificate (बिजली का बिल): Pump details or well registry. / कुएं या विद्युत कनेक्शन विवरण।
🌾

Who is Eligible? / पात्रता की शर्तें

1
Minimum Land / न्यूनतम भूमि

At least 0.2 hectares of irrigated land is required for subsidy application. / आवेदन के लिए कम से कम 0.2 हेक्टेयर भूमि अनिवार्य।

2
Small/Marginal/Women / विशेष प्राथमिकता

Small, marginal, and women farmers receive higher subsidy rates (70% to 75%). / लघु, सीमांत व महिला किसानों को विशेष वरीयता।

3
Khet Talai (Farm Pond) / खेत तलाई योजना

Subsidies for lined ponds up to Rs. 90,000 to prevent seepage of rainwater. / प्लास्टिक शीट तलाई पर अधिकतम ₹90,000 तक सब्सिडी।
