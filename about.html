Our Mission

Built For The Rajasthan Farmer

Mithari Agro Agency was founded to solve a very real problem: farmers across Rajasthan were losing thousands of liters of water every day to inefficient irrigation methods, seeping ponds, and outdated piping.

We set out to change that — by bringing quality water infrastructure products, honest technical guidance, and reliable after-sales service directly to farms and project sites across the region.

"Our goal is not simply to sell products, but to help build resilient agricultural ecosystems capable of supporting future generations of Rajasthan farmers."

Our Journey

Growing Together With Rajasthan's Farmers

Year 2000 — Founding

Mithari Agro Agency Established

Founded by Dharam Chand Jain in Mithari with a clear vision — to make quality agricultural water infrastructure accessible to every farmer in Rajasthan.

Expansion Phase

HDPE Pipeline & Pond Lining Added

Expanded product catalog to include HDPE pipe networks and geomembrane pond lining solutions, fulfilling growing demand for water storage.

Regional Growth

Expanded Across 15+ Districts

Supply chains and partnerships grew to serve farmers and contractors across 15+ Rajasthan districts, including Nagaur, Jodhpur, and Sikar.

Quality Milestone

ISI Certification & Brand Partnerships

Partnered with leading national manufacturers carrying ISI certification, ensuring consistent product quality and regulatory compliance.

Today & Beyond

25,000+ Farmers Served Across 25+ Districts

25 years later, Mithari Agro Agency is Rajasthan's most trusted name in agricultural water infrastructure — still led by its founding family, still farmer-first in every decision.
